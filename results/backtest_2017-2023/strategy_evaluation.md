### A. The "Distribution Chop" (Nov-Dec 2021)
**Pattern:** Price oscillates around the EMA 200 with no clear direction.
- **Risk:** The EMA 200 is flat (sideways). Price constantly sweeps and reclaims levels but fails to sustain momentum.
- **Result:** Consecutive small losses (-1R). This is the "Cost of Doing Business."

**Pattern:** Strong pump followed immediately by a stronger dump (e.g., Events driven news).
- **Risk:** The strategy enters on the reclaim, but the trend immediately collapses.
- **Result:** Stopped out instantly.
